Our Process

We offer more than just website design. We also develop a comprehensive digital strategy. The process we follow is intended to transform your organization online. Web or application projects involve art, advertising, messaging, branding, technology, and teamwork. While providing the best possible outcome, we also strive to make it as seamless and transparent as possible for our clients.

Strategy

It is this phase of our digital process that is probably the most critical to your project. During this phase, we gain a deep understanding of your organization, vision, and goals.

Creative

Designing a website to meet your goals starts at the creative phase, translating the brand identity into online creations.

Design

After defining your brand identity, ideas, and values, we use artistic inspiration to create the design of your website. This step is highly collaborative and interactive. We will be in constant communication with you to revise the plan based on your ideas and input.

Content

Messaging and design must work together to communicate your brand identity, so it is only natural that these phases occur concurrently.

After you complete the creative phase, you’ll have the design and message to serve as the basis for a functional website.

Development

As soon as your creative design is complete, our development team will create a functional website for you.

Testing

The zero flaw goal we aim to reach is to build you a perfect website or app. Everything we design and build is put through a battery of tests to verify that all functions are implemented and your website or app renders perfectly on all devices.

Launch

Data and content migrations are finalised, hosting is installed, and your website goes live on the production servers. Once the project is live on the internet, we perform a final analysis to ensure that everything is running smoothly.

Evaluation

As your site is launched, we then test its functionality with live visitors. We use cutting-edge analytics tools to assess the site’s performance and visitor behaviour.
